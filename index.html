WHAT IS THE LANNA SCHOOL SHOP?

The Lanna Snack Shop is run by Lanna International School students learning Enterprise, an IGCSE similar to Business Studies. As a project, they sell all sorts of goods, such as chocolate croissants, pizza and pretzel bread for students to enjoy. It is run at both breaktime and lunchtime, catering to all. Sold are also drinks, such as chocolate milk and some sodas.

Sold at the snackshop are the following:

  • Chocolate croissants
  • Pizza
  • Pretzel bread buns
  • Chocolate
  • Moo ping, otherwise known as pork kebabs
